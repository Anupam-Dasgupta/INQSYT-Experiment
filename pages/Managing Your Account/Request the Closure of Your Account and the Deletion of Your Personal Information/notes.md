### Observations for Request the Closure of Your Account and the Deletion of Your Personal Information

- **Intent Additions**:
  - Added `close_account` as the primary customer capability described on this page, which covers both the self-service web flow and the email fallback mechanism for users without accounts or authorized agents.
- **Excluded Details**:
  - Verification requirements (replying to email/text within five days) and consequences of closure (inability to restore/access products and services) are treated as policy details/instructions rather than distinct customer intents.
  - Redirect link to "Why Can't I Log into My Account?" is excluded as an external page redirect.
