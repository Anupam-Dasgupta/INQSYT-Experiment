### Observations for Request the Closure of Your Account and the Deletion of Your Personal Information

- **Page Summary**: This page contains instructions for users requesting the closure of their Amazon account and permanent deletion of their personal information.
- **Intent Consolidation**: The intents `close_amazon_account` (for registered account holders using the online form) and `request_account_closure_as_non_holder` (for non-account holders or authorized agents using email) have been merged into `close_account` to represent the unified capability of requesting account closure and data deletion.
- **Edge Cases**: Note that closing the account impacts all products and services globally using those credentials, and requires email/text verification within 5 days.
