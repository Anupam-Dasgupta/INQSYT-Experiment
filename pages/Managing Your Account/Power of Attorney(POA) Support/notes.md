### Observations for Power of Attorney(POA) Support

- **Intent Additions**: Added `request_poa_support` to represent the single unified action for contacting Power of Attorney support via email to request account access or handle an account on behalf of the account owner.
- **Excluded Details**:
  - Direct account sign-in/access details are excluded as they represent generic account actions rather than POA-specific goals.
  - Recommended help topics (Bereavement Support, How to Obtain Amazon Transaction Records After Identity Theft, Amazon and Your Personal Information, How Amazon Protects Your Personal Information, How Amazon Uses Your Personal Information) are excluded as external page redirects.
