### Observations for Bereavement Support

- **Intent Additions**: Added `request_bereavement_support` to represent the single unified action for contacting bereavement support via email to request account deactivation, closure, or changes when an account holder passes away.
- **Excluded Details**:
  - Direct account sign-in/access details are excluded as they represent generic account actions rather than bereavement-specific goals.
  - The password reset guide is excluded as it redirects to an external help article.
  - Recommended help topics (Power of Attorney Support, Account Closure request page) are excluded as external page redirects.
